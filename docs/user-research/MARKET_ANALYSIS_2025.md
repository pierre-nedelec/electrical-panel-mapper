### Leading Competitors & Solutions

#### **1. CircuitIQ - Market Leader**
**Position:** Premium professional solution with patented hardware
- **Capability:** Map and label circuits at **15,000 sq ft per day**
- **Accuracy:** **100% accuracy** with hardware integration
- **Target:** High-end professional electricians and large facilities
- **Advantage:** Patented mapping hardware with proven track record

#### **2. Circuit Mapper Systems** 
**Position:** Professional multi-circuit testing
- **Capability:** Handle **42-84 branch circuits concurrently**
- **Target:** Professional electrical contractors
- **Advantage:** Bulk circuit testing capability

#### **3. Traditional Tools**
**Position:** Basic manual solutions
- **Tools:** Circuit testers, lamps, cable tracers
- **Limitations:** Manual, time-intensive, accuracy issues
- **User feedback:** "Incredibly inaccurate and frustrating to use"

#### **4. Software-Only Solutions**
**Position:** Design and documentation
- **Examples:** SmartDraw, Circuit Diagram software
- **Focus:** Creating diagrams rather than actual circuit mapping
- **Gap:** No integration with physical circuit identification

### Market Gap Analysis

#### **üî¥ Critical Pain Points Identified:**

1. **Time & Complexity**
   - Manual mapping "is not an easy task to perform - takes a lot of time"
   - Traditional methods require multiple people
   - Extensive manual testing required

2. **Accuracy & Reliability**
   - False positives from existing tools
   - Difficulty getting clear readings
   - Problems with hard-to-reach outlets

3. **Documentation & Maintenance**
   - Many homes lack proper circuit references on panels
   - No digital records that update with changes
   - Compliance and safety reporting challenges
